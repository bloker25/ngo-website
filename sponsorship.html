Frequently Asked Questions

How does sponsorship work?

Your monthly contribution directly supports your sponsored beneficiary through our structured programs. You'll receive regular updates and can communicate with them through our supervised channels.

Can I visit my sponsored child/family?

Yes, we arrange supervised visits following our child protection policies and scheduling procedures.

How long does sponsorship last?

We encourage long-term commitments to provide stability, but you can adjust or end your sponsorship at any time.

How are my contributions used?

85% of sponsorship funds go directly to beneficiary support, with 15% covering essential administrative and program management costs.
