Our Mission

Street Children

Providing shelter, education, healthcare, and most importantly, love and guidance to children who have nowhere else to turn. Every child deserves a chance at a brighter future.

Vulnerable Women

Supporting women in crisis situations with emergency shelter, job training, counseling, and empowerment programs to help them rebuild their lives with dignity.

Hospital Patients

Visiting and caring for patients who have no family or friends, providing companionship, basic necessities, and emotional support during their most vulnerable moments.

Forgotten Prisoners

Offering rehabilitation programs, family reunification services, and post-release support for inmates who have been abandoned by their families and communities.

Making a Real Impact

Founded on the belief that no human being should face life's challenges alone, Elayon Mountain Foundation has been a beacon of hope for society's most vulnerable members since our inception.

Our dedicated team of volunteers and social workers work tirelessly to identify and support those who have fallen through society's cracks. We don't just provide temporary relief – we build lasting relationships and create pathways to sustainable change.

Through our comprehensive programs, we've learned that sometimes the smallest gesture – a warm meal, a listening ear, or simply showing up – can transform a life.

Maria's New Beginning

"I was living on the streets with my two children when Elayon Mountain Foundation found us. They didn't just give us a place to sleep – they gave us a future. Today, I have a job, my children are in school, and we have our own apartment. They saw potential in me when I couldn't see it myself."

Hope City
August 2025

James' Recovery Journey

"After my surgery, I had no one. My family had given up on me years ago. The volunteers from Elayon Mountain Foundation visited me every day, brought me books, helped me with rehabilitation exercises. They showed me that someone cared. That made all the difference in my recovery."

Central Hospital

Alex's Second Chance

"When I was released from prison, I had nowhere to go. Elayon Mountain Foundation helped me find housing, connected me with job training, and most importantly, helped me reconnect with my daughter. I'm now a certified electrician and have custody of my child again."
